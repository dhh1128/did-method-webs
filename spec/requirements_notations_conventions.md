## Requirements, Notation and Conventions

The following key words in this document are to be interpreted as
described in BCP 14 [RFC2119] [RFC8174] when, and only when, they
appear in all capitals, as shown here:
1. "MUST"
1. "MUST NOT"
1. "REQUIRED"
1. "SHALL"
1. "SHALL NOT"
1. "SHOULD"
1. "SHOULD NOT"
1. "RECOMMENDED"
1. "NOT RECOMMENDED"
1. "MAY"
1. "OPTIONAL"
